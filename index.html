Insurance · Revenue Recovery

Dormant Policy Remarketing at Scale

Most insurance organizations have thousands of dormant accounts — lapsed policies, unconverted quotes, and past clients who went quiet. Manually re-engaging them is expensive and inconsistent. We build AI remarketing systems that identify high-intent re-engagement windows, generate personalized outreach, and route warm responses to your producers automatically.

  • AI identifies dormant accounts by lapse date, policy type, and re-engagement probability
  • Personalized multi-channel sequences deployed without manual effort
  • Warm responses routed to the right producer with full context
  • Integrates with your existing AMS or CRM — no data migration required

Trades & Field Services · Operational Efficiency

AI-Powered Service Quoting for Field Teams

Field service companies lose hours every day to manual quoting — technicians calling back to the office, coordinators building estimates from scratch, and customers waiting days for a number. We build AI quoting systems that let field teams generate accurate, approved quotes on-site using a mobile interface backed by your pricing logic and job history.

Industrial & Manufacturing · Revenue Acceleration

Complex Service Quoting for Industrial Organizations

Industrial service organizations deal with multi-variable quoting — equipment specifications, compliance requirements, parts availability, and custom labour configurations. We build AI quoting platforms that ingest RFQs, cross-reference your technical database, and produce accurate quotes in minutes rather than days — giving your sales team a competitive edge on response time.

  • RFQ ingested via email, portal, or direct input — AI parses specifications automatically
  • Cross-references parts database, compliance requirements, and historical job data
  • Draft quote generated for engineer review — not from scratch, from a strong baseline
  • Quote history and win/loss data feeds back into AI to improve accuracy over time
